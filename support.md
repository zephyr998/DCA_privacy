# Support

**Last updated:** 2026-04-26

## Need help?

If you run into a problem, have a question, or want to suggest a feature, you have two ways to reach us:

1. **Leave a review or comment on the App Store.** We read every review and use them to prioritize fixes and new features.
2. **Email us** at **offlinehelp2025@gmail.com**. We aim to respond within a few business days.

When emailing, it helps to include:

- A short description of what you were trying to do
- What happened vs. what you expected
- Your iPhone model and iOS version (Settings → General → About)
- The app version (visible in the App Store listing)
- A screenshot if the issue is visual

## Common questions

**Where does the price data come from?**
DCA fetches publicly available historical prices from Yahoo Finance. Calculations are run entirely on your device.

**Why is a particular ticker missing?**
The app supports the S&P 500, selected NASDAQ-100 names, several major ETFs (SPY, QQQ, VOO, VTI, IVV, DIA, IWM, SMH, TQQQ, SOXL), and BTC-USD. The S&P 500 list refreshes from a public dataset every time you open the app. If a ticker you expect is missing, let us know.

**Why does my calculation fail or show "no data"?**
This usually means the price provider didn't return data for the selected date range — for example, the start date is before the asset existed, or the network request was blocked. Try a more recent start date or check your internet connection.

**Can I export my results?**
Not yet. Saved results currently live on your device only. If exporting (CSV, share sheet, etc.) would be useful to you, please send a request via email.

**Does the app collect any personal data?**
No. Everything stays on your device. See the [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md) for details.

**Is this financial advice?**
No. DCA is an educational backtesting tool. Past performance does not guarantee future results, and nothing in the app constitutes investment advice.
